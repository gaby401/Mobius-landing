Why Mobius?

Lightweight Install

Install the blockchain directly on your phone with a compact APK. No bloated downloads — start transacting in seconds.

Fast & Secure

Experience high‑speed transactions backed by battle‑tested cryptography and a modular proof‑of‑stake consensus.

Real‑World Ready

Built‑in NFC support enables tap‑to‑pay for everyday purchases. MOBI tokens are capped and burnable for sustainable economics.

Extensible Design

Add new features and modules as your needs evolve — from custom dApps to validator tools — without reinstalling the app.

Users

Get Started

Download APK

Grab the latest Mobius APK from our releases page and sideload it on any modern Android device.

Mint $MOBI

Use the built‑in wallet to mint your first MOBI tokens and explore the ecosystem.

Tap to Pay

Send and receive payments with a simple tap using NFC technology — no QR codes or wallets needed.

Run a Validator

Operate a full validator node on Android and earn rewards while securing the network.
